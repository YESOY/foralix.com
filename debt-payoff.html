Debt Snowball vs Avalanche Methods

The Debt Snowball method focuses on paying off debts from smallest to largest balance. This creates quick wins and psychological motivation as you eliminate debts one by one.

The Debt Avalanche method prioritizes debts with the highest interest rates first. This approach saves you the most money on interest over time but may take longer to see progress.

Which Method Should You Choose?

Choose Snowball If:

You need motivation, want quick wins, have similar interest rates, or struggle with debt burnout.

Choose Avalanche If:

You want to save the most money, have high-interest debts, or are highly disciplined.
